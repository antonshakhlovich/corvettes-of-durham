​Corvettes  of  Durham
​​Corvettes of Durham was established in 2000 by a dedicated group of Corvette enthusiasts.  Our General Meetings are held monthly, every third Wednesday at 5:30 pm at several different locations. During the snow free months we arrange cruises to various locations and also attend car shows and events in Southern Ontario. The club sponsors 2 charities,  our main charity is the Oshawa Cancer Care Centre (Lakeridge Health) and to date we have donated $52,600.00  Our newest charity is the Humane Society of Durham Region. If you require more information about the club and club membership please contact membership at corvettesofdurham@outlook.com
